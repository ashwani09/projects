MATT CLARK MUSIC

Fun, quirky music for the whole family.

Matt writes and performs kids' music in Portland where he's become a favourite on the blossoming Kindle music scene. Children and parents alike love his funny, off beat songs, inspired by his Own experience as a father to two young boys.

Matt's first album, "funny little fella" was listed as one of the best kids' music debuts of 2008 by about.com. His follow up album was hailed by Oregon music news as a step above his first - "more variety tighter song craft, great production yet still the same gentle, loving sentiments and approach to kids."
